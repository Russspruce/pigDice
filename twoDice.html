  • Roll the dice, seeing how many points you can get.
  • If you roll a 'one', then you lose your points for that round and it's the next player's turn.
  • If you roll doubles, the dice are multiplied and added to your total.
  • If you roll double ones, you lose all your points.
  • Select 'Stop' to save points and add them to the total score.
  • First player to '100 points' wins the game!
